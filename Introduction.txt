Welcome to the Kahoot Trivia Simulator! 

Unlike most trivas, we offer you the ability to pick a funky cool player name
from our uniquely generated list, and answer world renowed trivia questions.
Here, you will also be able to add, search, and or delete from our list of
nicknames to prevent your opponents from chosing a cooler name than you!

Each player will pick a player nickname, and then proceed to answer 5 triva 
questions. After each question points will be awarded and a scoreboard will be 
displayed. The objective is to get the most amount of questions right to WIN!
